2. Llms.txt Implementation

ℹ️ Emerging Standard — Create /llms.txt

  • File flagged as missing in SEMrush AI Search audit
  • Llms.txt tells AI crawlers (GPT, Claude, Gemini) which pages to reference for AI answers
  • Include: about page, course pages, key blog articles, FAQ pages, contact
  • Exclude: account/login pages, terms pages, duplicate content

3. Conversational Content Architecture

  • Add data tables comparing Islamic trading accounts — AI models prefer structured data
  • Embed author bios with credentials on every article — E-E-A-T signals for AI models
